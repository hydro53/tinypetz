About the collection

Every pet tells
a story.

Tiny Pets explores the bond between owner and companion. Each piece is an algorithmically generated portrait — unique traits, moods, and accessories that make every pet feel alive and personal.

No two are alike. The algorithm uses over 200 parameters to define each pet's look, personality, and rarity.

FAQ

Questions
answered.

What blockchain is Tiny Pets on?+
Tiny Pets is deployed on Ethereum mainnet. All metadata and artwork is stored on-chain, meaning your NFT will exist as long as Ethereum does.
How many can I mint per wallet?+
During the whitelist period, wallets can mint up to 2 per transaction. During public mint, the limit is 5 per wallet across all transactions.
Is every Tiny Pet unique?+
Yes. Each Tiny Pet is generated deterministically from its token ID using our generative algorithm. No two tokens share the same traits, accessories, or mood.
What are the commercial rights?+
Holders receive full commercial rights to their individual Tiny Pet. You can print, sell, or use your NFT's artwork however you like.
Will there be future drops?+
Yes. Tiny Pets holders will receive priority access to all future collections and collaborative drops. Holding a Tiny Pet is your membership to the wider ecosystem.
Where can I trade after minting?+
Tiny Pets will be listed on OpenSea, Blur, and LooksRare immediately after the public mint. Secondary royalties are set at 5%.
